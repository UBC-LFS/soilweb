Meet Vancouver's Soils

The soils of Vancouver were formed thousands of years ago through dynamic processes involving ice, salt water and fresh water. These processes have caused different soils to develop in different areas of the city. Vancouver’s soils can be categorized into four distinct soil management groups. Information about these soils and their unique characteristics is important for anyone who is interested in managing their soils effectively.
