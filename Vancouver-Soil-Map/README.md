### Google map key

The **Google map key** is required to enable the Google map.


### KML files

There are 4 kml files to display 4 different colours on the Google map.

- Bose-Heron.kml
- Delta- Tsawwassen.kml
- Langley-Cloverdale.kml
- Whatcom-Scat.kml
